About the Book

Many children face different fears as they grow up and are never taught how to have victory over them. Be Gone, Fear is a book that encourages children to overcome those fears and to live a life filled with courage.

About the author

Breanna Robinson, the author of Be Gone, Fear, is a young adult from C3 Church San Diego who leads in the children’s ministry, C3 Kidz. She is passionate about building the church and building up the people who will truly change the city and this world. Each experience as a pastor’s child has taught her the importance of making sure people know their identity in Christ and God's purpose for their lives.

As a new author, her heart is not for there to be thousands of books sold, but for there to be thousands of lives changed. Join her in building up the next generation and equipping them to carry boldness and choose faith over fear.
