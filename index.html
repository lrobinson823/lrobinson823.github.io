About Me

Hi, I'm Lamar.

I am a frontend developer interested in making robust and innovative web applications. I have always had a inclination for technology and for discovering how technology works. Curiousity has been the driving force behind my desire for knowledge.

I am currently working as a student mentor at Codify Academy, a frontend bootcamp in San Francisco. When I'm not working on projects, I enjoy longboarding, attending meetups to discuss the latest and greatest trends in web development and listening to jazz.

My aspirations lie in working on projects that I am passionate about and finding the best ways possible to showcase them to the world. I wholeheartedly believe that a developers greatest satisfaction is to not only actualize a product that they themselves enjoy, but to also share their creation with others.
