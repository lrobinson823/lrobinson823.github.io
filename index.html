About

I am a Front End Developer interested in making robust and innovative web applications. I have always had an inclination for technology and for discovering how technology works. Curiosity has been the driving force behind my desire for knowledge.

I am a graduate of Codify Academy, a Front End Web Developer Bootcamp in San Francisco and have recently been working remotely for a sass company as an Associate Web Developer / Web Specialist based in Los Angeles. When I'm not working on projects, I enjoy attending meetups to discuss the latest and greatest trends in web development, longboarding, traveling and listening to hip-hop and jazz music.

My aspirations lie in working on projects that I am passionate about and finding the best ways possible to showcase them to the world. I wholeheartedly believe that a developer's greatest satisfaction is to not only actualize a product that they themselves enjoy, but to also share their creation with others.
