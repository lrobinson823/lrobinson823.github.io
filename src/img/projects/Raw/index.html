Christian Scott

American Jazz Trumpeter

New Orleans, LA

RAW Artist since Feb 2019

About

Christian Scott (born March 31, 1983, in New Orleans, Louisiana), also known as Christian Scott aTunde Adjuah, is an American jazz trumpeter, composer and producer. Scott is the nephew of jazz saxophonist Donald Harrison. Scott has won the Edison Award twice and has been Grammy nominated twice. Christian Scott is known for his signature 'whisper technique', noted as emphasizing breath over vibration at the mouth piece creating a unique tone. Scott's debut album Rewind That (2006) was released with Concord Records. It received a Grammy nomination. Scott received the Edison Award in 2010 and 2012. Since 2002, Scott has released 12 studio albums, and two live recordings.
